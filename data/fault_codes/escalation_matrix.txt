FAULT ESCALATION MATRIX
Plant: Bengaluru Manufacturing Unit

RISK LEVEL DEFINITIONS
- Low      : Monitor only. Log in system. No immediate action required.
- Medium   : Attend within 8 hours. Notify shift supervisor.
- High     : Attend within 4 hours. Notify maintenance manager.
- Critical : Immediate shutdown. Notify plant manager and safety officer.

ESCALATION CONTACTS
- Shift Supervisor    : Available on plant radio channel 3
- Maintenance Manager : Available 06:00 to 22:00
- Plant Manager       : Available for Critical faults only
- OEM Support Hotline : For warranty equipment faults

RESPONSE TIME TARGETS
- Critical : 15 minutes response, 1 hour resolution
- High     : 1 hour response, 4 hour resolution
- Medium   : 4 hour response, 8 hour resolution
- Low      : Next shift response, 24 hour resolution

DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
- All faults must be logged in maintenance management system
- Root cause analysis required for all High and Critical faults
- Repeat faults within 30 days require engineering review
