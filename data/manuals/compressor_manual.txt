INDUSTRIAL COMPRESSOR MANUAL
Model: CP-2000 Series
Manufacturer: AirTech Industries

SECTION 1 - NORMAL OPERATING PARAMETERS
- Inlet Pressure  : 14.5 PSI (atmospheric)
- Outlet Pressure : 160 to 185 PSI (normal operating)
- Pressure Warning: below 140 PSI or above 200 PSI
- Temperature     : 70 to 90 degree C (normal)
- Oil Level       : between MIN and MAX mark on dipstick
- RPM             : 1450 to 1500 RPM

SECTION 2 - FAULT CODES
Fault Code CP-01: Pressure Drop
  Cause: Air leak in pipeline, faulty pressure relief valve, worn piston rings
  Symptoms: Output pressure drops below 140 PSI suddenly
  Action: Inspect all pipeline joints. Check pressure relief valve. Measure piston ring gap.

Fault Code CP-05: High Discharge Temperature
  Cause: Insufficient cooling, high ambient temperature, dirty cooler
  Symptoms: Discharge temperature above 100 degree C
  Action: Clean intercooler and aftercooler fins. Check cooling water flow.

Fault Code CP-12: Oil Contamination in Air Line
  Cause: Worn piston rings, excessive oil level, wrong oil grade
  Symptoms: Oil mist in compressed air output
  Action: Replace piston rings. Check and correct oil level. Use recommended oil grade CP-VG100.

Fault Code CP-18: Compressor Surge
  Cause: Sudden drop in flow demand, inlet valve malfunction
  Symptoms: Rapid pressure fluctuation, unusual noise
  Action: Check inlet valve operation. Install anti-surge control if not present.

SECTION 3 - MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
- Daily   : Check oil level, drain moisture from receiver tank
- Weekly  : Inspect belts, clean air filter
- Monthly : Change oil filter, check safety valve
- Yearly  : Replace piston rings, full overhaul
